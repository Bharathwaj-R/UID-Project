About Restraunt

After Hungsters was born, cheat days were considered long gone. From a 25 sq. ft red kiosk to a fully established outlet with top-notch ambience and groovy tunes playing in the background, we sure have come a long way. In an era where people use their favourite fast-food dish as a reward on a cheat day, Hungsters strives to stand out by being your go-to spot for fresh, light and guilt-free food any day!
